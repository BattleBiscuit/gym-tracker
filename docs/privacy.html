Privacy Policy

PRsonal · Last updated: June 2025

Overview

PRsonal is a personal gym tracker. All data you enter — workouts, exercises, sets, weights, training plans — is stored exclusively on your device using your device's local storage. No data is ever transmitted to any server, and no account is required.

Data We Collect

We collect no personal data. Specifically:

  • No names, email addresses, or account information
  • No location data
  • No health or biometric data beyond what you manually enter
  • No analytics, crash reports, or usage telemetry
  • No advertising identifiers

Data Storage

All workout data is stored locally on your device using IndexedDB (a standard browser/app storage API). This data never leaves your device unless you use the built-in Export feature, which creates a file saved directly to your device's storage.

Network Usage

The app uses the internet solely to check for updates by querying the GitHub Releases API. No personal data is sent during this request. This check only occurs when you manually tap "Check for Updates" in Settings.

Permissions

  • Notifications — used to alert you when your rest timer ends, even if the screen is off. No notification content is transmitted anywhere.
  • Wake Lock — used to keep the screen on during an active workout session.
  • Internet — used only for the optional update check described above.

Third-Party Services

PRsonal does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-sharing services.

Children

PRsonal does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new date. Since no data is collected, changes are unlikely to affect you.
